Leadership

Don Baker

Founder, CIO

Don created Brainn Wave after a career working with complex and secure data. Realising the power that can come from accessing the right data at the right time, he wanted to use his experience to help break down barriers for others to harness this power. After his earlier attempts at being a professional tennis player didn't work out, helping to solve the worlds Grand Challenges seemed like the next best thing.

Steve Coates

Co-Founder, CEO

Steve joined forces with Don in May 2015. Over a steak and a coke, Don painted his vision for a world where data is power and by breaking down barriers to access that data you have the opportunity to completely change the world. After a career helping to build other people’s businesses, this represented a welcome change. Sealing the deal by splitting the bill 50/50, at the time he had no idea it would lead to him relocating his family to Scotland and taking up mountain biking (or pushing a bike up a hill) as a new hobby.

Graham Jones

Co-Founder, CTO

Graham very quickly completed the trinity of the executive team. Living in Australia at the time, a 30 minute Skype, in which we “had him at hello”, flipped his life upside down - literally. Within a few months he relocated to the other side of the world; a long way from our very first call with investors where Graham had to dial in from his car with his heaters on full blast in the middle of the night at a ski resort in Australia. His 20 year career building large complicated data systems had all been leading up to this point. When he’s not coding he is either riding horses, climbing up mountains or skiing down them.

Gordon Fitzjohn

Co-Founder, Chairman

Gordon was previously Global Head and Managing Director at Deutsche Bank Corporate Trust with 35 years’ experience in the finance sector. As an active technology investor, Gordon has specific interests in the FinTech sector. Gordon channels his entrepreneurial spirit into Pollen – Velocity Capital, where he is chairman and co-founder. The company aims to expedite growth of digital businesses.

Nigel Peters

Co-Founder, NED

Nigel is managing partner and CEO of Alium Partners Ltd, a niche interim management recruitment and consultancy company. He is a change specialist working on sensitive specialist IT programmes. Nigel has had a full career in the UK military, leading and commanding a number of the RAF's military forces.

Andy Jarrett

Co-Founder, NED

Andy is the CEO of Twelve40, a global platform providing lottery and social gaming solutions. He has previously been director at Digital Jersey which leads the island’s digital transformation including economic and reputational objectives. Andy has previously implemented strategy for hosting managed services and commerce solutions which were delivered to 30 countries.
